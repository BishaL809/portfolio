01 — Roots

Born in the Hills

Growing up in Meghalaya, surrounded by rolling hills and slow internet — which, honestly, made me obsess over efficiency in apps. If something took too long to load, I wanted to fix it.

Meghalaya, Northeast India

That curiosity pulled me toward computers. Not just using them — building with them.

02 — The Spark
⚡

BCA & the Flutter Revelation

Three years at North East Hill University doing BCA. First year: confused. Second year: hooked on Flutter. Third year: shipping code.

2022–2025 · BCA

Flutter clicked for me because one codebase could reach millions — Android, iOS, the whole world. That kind of leverage felt like a superpower.

03 — The Why
💪

Why I Built FitHub

Simple problem: I kept missing workouts and losing streaks. No app felt personal enough. Most were bloated, paid, or ugly.

FitHub — Born from frustration

So I built what I wanted: a clean, fast fitness tracker with streak motivation, day-wise plans, and real-time Firebase sync. No subscriptions. Just consistency.
