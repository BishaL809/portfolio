01 — Roots
🌄

Hills, Slow Internet & Big Dreams

Meghalaya, Northeast India

Grew up in Meghalaya — lush hills, monsoon rain, notoriously slow internet. That frustration made me care deeply about how apps feel. If a button lagged, I noticed. That sensitivity turned into obsession, and obsession turned into code.

02 — The Spark
⚡

Flutter Clicked. Everything Changed.

BCA · 2022–2025 · NEHU

Three years at North East Hill University. First year confused. Second year — discovered Flutter. One codebase, two platforms, infinite possibilities. Shipping my first app on Android and iOS simultaneously felt like a cheat code.

03 — The Why
💪

FitHub Wasn't an Assignment. It Was a Problem.

Built out of frustration

Every fitness app was paywalled, bloated, or just ugly. None felt like mine. So I built FitHub — not because a professor told me to, but because I genuinely needed it. Streaks, day-wise plans, real-time Firebase sync. Clean. Fast. Personal.
